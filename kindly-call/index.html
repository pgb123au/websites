Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really AI? Will my parent know?

Yes, Kindly Call uses advanced AI voice technology. We're transparent about this - during signup, we help you explain it to your loved one. The AI sounds natural, warm, and caring. Many seniors enjoy their calls and look forward to them daily!

What if my parent doesn't answer?

If there's no answer, we'll try again after a few minutes. If we still can't reach them, you'll receive an instant alert so you can check in. You decide what happens next.

Can they use a landline?

Absolutely! Kindly Call works with any phone - landline or mobile. No apps or special equipment needed.

What if my parent has hearing difficulties?

Our AI voice speaks clearly and can be adjusted for hearing aids. You can also add notes about hearing preferences, and we'll adapt the conversation accordingly.

Is my family's data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted and stored securely in Australian data centers. We comply with Australian privacy laws and never share your information.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! There are no lock-in contracts. You can pause or cancel your subscription anytime from your dashboard. If you cancel during your free trial, you won't be charged at all.

What happens in an emergency?

Our AI detects keywords like "fallen", "chest pain", or "need help". You'll receive an immediate SMS and notification. We'll guide you on next steps, including calling 000 if needed.

How does the free trial work?

Start your 14-day trial with no credit card required. You'll get full access to your chosen plan's features. Before the trial ends, we'll remind you to add payment if you'd like to continue.

Have more questions? Contact us - we're here to help!
