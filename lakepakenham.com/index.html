Pakenham's Beautiful Lakes

Lake Pakenham

Lakeside Recreation Reserve, Pakenham

The main recreational lake in Pakenham, officially known as Lakeside Recreation Reserve. This beautiful wetland reserve features a scenic lake perfect for family outings, fishing, and peaceful walks.

  • Wetland reserve with lake
  • Walking and cycling tracks
  • Birdwatching opportunities
  • Picnic and BBQ facilities
  • Playgrounds and dog-friendly areas

Lakeside Estate Lakes

Lakeside Estate, Pakenham

Beautiful lake within the Lakeside residential estate, featuring a gentle 1.5km loop around the lake. Popular for fishing (stocked with rainbow trout), sailing, and family recreation.

  • 1.5km walking loop around lake
  • Fishing - stocked with rainbow trout
  • Lakeside Sailing Club activities
  • Playgrounds and exercise areas
  • Free BBQ facilities and toilets
  • Abundant birdlife (swans, ducks)

Toomuc Recreation Reserve

Olympic Way, Pakenham

58.4-acre recreation reserve featuring access to nearby wetlands and Toomuc Creek. This natural area provides opportunities for walking, wildlife viewing, and enjoying nature in Pakenham.

  • 58.4-acre recreation reserve
  • Access to wetlands and creek
  • Walking trails through nature
  • Native wildlife habitat
  • Bird observation areas
  • Environmental education

Officer (Near Pakenham)

  • Scenic lake views
  • Walking and cycling paths
  • Modern parklands
  • Playground facilities

Cardinia Reservoir

Emerald (Near Pakenham)

Melbourne's second-largest reservoir, located in Emerald near Pakenham. The 287,000 ML reservoir is surrounded by Cardinia Reservoir Park, offering spectacular recreational facilities and over 40km of walking trails.

  • 287,000 ML reservoir (2nd largest)
  • 440-hectare surrounding park
  • 40km of walking trails
  • Panoramic lookout points
  • Visitor center and facilities
  • Mountain biking tracks
  • Kangaroo viewing trail
